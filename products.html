ALICE Speaks

The AliceSpeaks™ application identifies and translates objects in the environment to multiple different languages through the use of machine learning. Users are able to tap on the speech button to have the identified item stated out loud in English and translated in the user-selected language of choice.

Learn More

ALICE Thinks

The AliceThinks™ application leverages the power of spatial computing and machine learning to identify objects. Simply by tapping on the object in the camera view, the application will display an AR 3D model of the word specified in the real world space.
